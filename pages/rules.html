Deadline

All entries must be completed by 10 January 2020 for collection week commencing 13 January 2020.

Theme

Theme for 2019/2020 is 'My Reading'.

Categories

  • Reception to year 2
  • Years 3 to 4
  • Years 5 to 6
  • Years 7 to 9
  • SEN Moderate
  • SEN Severe
  • SEN Profound

Materials

Oil, watercolour, pastel, crayon, pencil, poster paints or ink on Paper Only.

Size of paper

  • A4 or A3 (no collage).
  • Pictures should NOT be mounted.
  • Please note that we recommend rectangular sized and good quality paper (not A4 printer paper) as these photograph and reproduce much better for postcards and canvases.

Entry Fee

Â£2 per picture.

Labelling of work

  • Name, school year or SEN category and school must be clearly marked in block capitals at the back of each entry.
  • There should be no name or signature on the front of the picture.
  • A short explanation may be put on the back of the picture which we will try to include if the picture is selected.
  • Each school should provide a complete list of the children and their year group, together with a summary of the numbers of pictures submitted per age category.

Selection

The number of pictures selected from each school is a proportion of the total submitted by that school, so the more entries there are from your school, the more pictures there will be exhibited.

Winning entries

Judges will select winners and runners up in each category.

Return of pictures

  • If your picture has not been selected, it will be returned via your school rep on the opening night.
  • If your picture has been selected and bought at the exhibition by your family, it will be returned to you via your school after the exhibition.
  • If your picture has been selected but has not been bought at the exhibition, we will not be able to return it. This is to encourage exhibitors and their parents to support the event by buying the pictures for a non-specified donation.

Condition for entering the competition

If your picture is selected for the exhibition, you agree to donate your picture to Young Art Reading for the purpose of raising money for Cancer Research UK. It means that you grant Young Art Reading the right to:

  • Exhibit your picture at the exhibition.
  • Take photographs of it and publish those photographs on our website and programme.
  • Sell this picture itself in return for a donation to Cancer Research UK, with a priority right for your family and friends.
  • Copy your picture on postcards or canvases and sell those products for the purpose of raising money for Cancer Research UK.
