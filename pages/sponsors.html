Working in partnership with Young Art Reading

Local connections and partnerships will be vital to the success of the Young Art Reading competition and exhibition.

We would therefore love to hear from organisations who would like to support this innovative community project, helping us to raise vital funds for Cancer Research UK's Kids and Teen's campaign.

Some ideas of how organisations can support Young Art Reading include:

  • Sponsoring a specific aspect of the competition/exhibition (e.g. flyer/programme printing, prizes for winners, exhibition materials such as easels/mounts/frames, etc.)
  • Making a donation to secure advertising space in the Young Art Reading exhibition programme
  • Making a donation to Young Art Reading to maximise the money raised for Cancer Research UK

If you would like to find out more, please use the form provided on the Contact Us page or youngartreading@gmail.com
