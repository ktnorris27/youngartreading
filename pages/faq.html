What is Young Art?
Young Art Project is a volunteer led project, with support and guidance provided by a local contact at Cancer Research UK. The project encourages young people to express their creativity through art. Click here for more information.
What is Young Art Reading?
Young Art Reading (YAR) is brand new art competition and exhibition for children and young people in Berkshire. Read more here.
