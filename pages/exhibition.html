Where :

The Great Hall at University of Reading

The Great Hall is located at the University of Reading's historic London Road campus, the original home of the University in the heart of Reading.

A beautiful Grade 2 listed building opened in 1905, the impressive Great Hall is where thousands of University of Reading students attend their graduation ceremonies every year. The hall also plays host to a number of public events and performances, including some by national choirs and various University ensembles.

Click here for London Road Campus map - the Great Hall is shown as number 45

Travelling by foot / bus:

It takes approximately 15 minutes to walk from Reading Railway Station. If you don’t want to walk from the station, bus routes 20, 20a and 21 run from near the station to Kendrick Road every 7–8 minutes. Buses also stop on London Road (9), Kings Road (17), and between London Road and Bulmershe (no 13). The latest bus timetables are available from the Reading Buses website.
